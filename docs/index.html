Our Project

For our project we will be taking a look at some of the documents (mostly from 1947-1950) that pertain to the founding of Penn State Behrend.

We have compiled a list of documents from the Behrend Library archives that pertain to,in some way,the founding of this campus. These include:five board meetings,three letters,one speech and one memo.
